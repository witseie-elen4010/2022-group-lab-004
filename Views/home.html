Welcome to WordleVersus

Start by guessing a 5 letter word.

This word should be a valid word.

Letters will appear in different colours.

Green letters appear in the word and are in the right position.

Yellow letters appear in the word, but are in the wrong position.

Grey letters don't appear in the word.

Play alone or against friends.

Multiplayer will allow you to see the board of your opponent, although their word guesses will be masked.

The multiplayer mode will also allow one player to choose a word for the others to guess.
